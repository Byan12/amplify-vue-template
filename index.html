About
Prome
Game

Prome Game is a leading game publishing company headquartered in Shanghai, China, with a focus on bringing innovative mobile games to the Mainland China market. We are dedicated to delivering diverse gaming experiences by collaborating with both established and up-and-coming developers. Our goal is to introduce groundbreaking gameplay to a broad audience, creating a vibrant community of passionate players. We specialize in working with small to medium-sized enterprises and independent developers, providing them with tailored solutions to help their games succeed. From expert localization and market-specific adjustments to strategic promotion and monetization, Prome Game ensures that every title we publish resonates with local players while maximizing its revenue potential. At the heart of our mission is the desire to ignite the fire of game innovation. We believe in inspiring creativity, driving excellence, and fostering strong partnerships with developers. Together, we strive to leave a lasting mark on the gaming landscape, ensuring that each game reaches its full potential and provides players with unforgettable experiences.

WHAT WE DO

Investment & Incubation

We provide investments to game projects & development studios, help and support the studio growth, and offer planning and advice including studio establishment, company registry, equity distribution, corporate growth, etc.

Game Development

Based on preliminary planning and product positioning, we provide support to the game development and optimization process including marketing, language localization, testing, optimization strategy, localized content development, platform porting, etc.

Publishing & Promotion

We provide a comprehensive publishing strategy and implement it for all platforms. It includes qualification & licenses application, promotion planning, media promotion, KOL promotion, community operation, event exhibition, etc.

Marketing Operation

We provide all marketing and operation services including crowdfunding, social media & community operation, feedback collection & analysis, crossover cooperation, IP operation, etc.
